About Us

Our History

Since 2016, our coffee shop has evolved from a humble neighborhood place to a cherished community gathering spot, renowned for our dedication to servetop-notch coffee and fostering a warm, welcoming environment. Our growth has been guided by steadfast commitment to quality and a passion for bringing people together.

Our Philosophy

At our core, we believe that coffee is a catalyst for connection and community. Our mission is to create a cozy and inclusive atmosphere where everyone feels valued and at ease, while serving expertly crafted coffee made from responsibly sourced, high-quality beans. We strive to be a hub for meaningful moments and memories, one cup at a time.

Our Quality

With a passion for excellence, we dedicate ourselves to serving exceptional coffee in every cup. We meticulously choose the finest beans, employ cutting-edge brewing methods and train our skilled baristas to coax out the optimal flavours. Our goal is to craft a truly unforgettable coffee experience for you, one cup at a time.

Meet Our Team

Bodika Muleka

Bodika, the founder, is a coffee connoisseur and culinary artist who infuses her love and expertise into every detail of the shop. With a deep understanding of flavour profiles and a passion for quality, she has created a haven for coffee enthusiasts, where every sip reflects her dedication to the craft.

Kalombo Kachunga

Kalombo, our seasoned head barista, brings about over 10 years of expertise to the craft, meticulously perfecting every cup of coffee that leaves our counter. His dedication to precision and quality quarantees a consistency expectional brew, every time.
